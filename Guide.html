You will be going against zombie waves in survival mode

Zombie waves will start spawning and come at you

Use W A S D to move around the map

Move your mouse to rotate your character and left mouse button to shoot

After you survive the wave next wave will start spawning with increasing numbers up untill wave 7

Every 7 waves a boss zombie will appear that has an increased
amount of health along with the wave

After wave 7 zombies' count will restart but their health will keep going up

You can choose one of two characters with two different characterstics

Slinger gets his damage increased as you survive more waves but has fixed health

Tanky gets his health increased as you survive more waves but has fixed damage

Good Luck!
