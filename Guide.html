You will be going against zombie waves in survival mode

Zombie waves will start spawning and come at you

Use W A S D to move around the map

Move your mouse to rotate your character and left mouse button to shoot

After you survive the wave next wave will start spawning with increasing numbers

After each wave you gain a skill point which u can spend to increase your health or damage

Kill 10 zombies in a row without taking a hit to fill your combo meter to launch a special attack
